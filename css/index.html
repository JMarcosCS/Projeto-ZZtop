About Us:

  • ZZ Top is a band that mixes blues-rock with southern American rock, referred to as "That Little Ol' Band from Texas", formed in 1969 in Houston, Texas. The band's original members are Billy Gibbons (vocals and guitar), Dusty Hill (vocals and bass) and Frank Beard (drums), and are the only band to have all original members playing together for 50 years.

ZZtop Curiosities:

  • According to the book "Z.Z Top: Bad and Worldwide", the name was inspired by a poster from
    Texas bluesman named Z.Z. Hill. They also wanted a name that suggested the best ("top").
  • ZZ Top has sold more than 50 million albums worldwide, including 25 million albums in the United States alone
  • The band has had 8 Top 40 hits on the Billboard Hot 100, 6 No. 1 hits on the
    Mainstream Rock, and three MTV Video Music Awards.
  • On July 28, 2021, Dusty Hill passed away in his sleep at his home in Houston, Texas.
